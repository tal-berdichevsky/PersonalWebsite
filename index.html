HowBusyIsMarino.com

Development:September 2022 - November 2022

Presentation: November 13th, 2022

HowBusyIsMarino.com is a website summarizing Northeastern gymnasium activity over previous periods of time. It produces graphical predictions for future capacity at Marino in the form of a heat map(click plus button bellow). This allows users to plan their future workouts accordingly.

Utilizes HTML/Bootstrap and Flask, as well as AWS for web scraping and as a previous hosting solution (under https://howbusyismarino.com).
